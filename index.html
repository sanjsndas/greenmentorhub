Composting Basics

This comprehensive workshop covers everything you need to know to start a successful composting system, whether you live in a house or an apartment. Learn how to turn kitchen scraps and garden waste into nutrient-rich compost that enriches the soil and reduces landfill waste. You will also learn about composting methods, troubleshooting common problems, and balancing green and brown materials for optimal results.

Participants will receive informational handouts and resource guides to continue composting successfully beyond the workshop.

Indoor Plant Care

Explore the world of indoor plants in this detailed workshop that will help you choose the right plants for your environment and maintain their health year-round. Topics covered include ideal light exposures, watering frequency, humidity control, pruning, fertilizing, and pest identification and management.

Attendees will also learn how to propagate plants and troubleshoot common issues to keep their indoor jungles thriving.

Seasonal Garden Planning

Advance your garden's productivity and health with this workshop focused on detailed seasonal planning. Learn about crop rotation, soil health management, planting schedules, and incorporating native and perennial plants for low-maintenance success. This workshop also discusses pest and disease anticipation related to seasons and climate.

The workshop includes practical planners and checklists to guide your yearly planting activities for consistent growth and harvest.

Gardening Tips & Articles

Maximizing Your Urban Garden Space

Urban gardening allows you to turn small spaces into lush green retreats. Using vertical gardening, container planting, and window boxes, you can create a vibrant garden regardless of space constraints.

In this article, learn how to select plants suited to small spaces, create microclimates, incorporate multi-purpose plants, and design your garden for maximum yield and aesthetic appeal.

Water Conservation Techniques

Water is a precious resource. This guide explains methods like mulching to retain soil moisture, drip irrigation to target the roots efficiently, and rainwater harvesting to reduce reliance on municipal supplies.

Discover how to create rain gardens and select drought-resistant plants to maintain a beautiful and healthy garden with minimal water use, saving both the environment and your utility bill.

Choosing the Right Soil for Your Plants

Soil health is fundamental to plant growth. Learn how to test your soil's texture, pH, and nutrient content.

We'll guide you through ways to amend your soil with organic matter, minerals, and beneficial microorganisms to create an optimal environment for your plants to thrive.

Preventing Common Plant Diseases

Early prevention and control of plant diseases saves your garden from serious damage. Understand the causes and symptoms of the most common fungal, bacterial, and viral diseases.

This article covers best practices such as crop rotation, proper spacing for airflow, cleanliness, use of resistant varieties, and organic or chemical treatment options when needed.
